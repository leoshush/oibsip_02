Atal Bihari Vajpayee

10th Prime Minister Of India

"अँधेरा छटेगा, सूरज निकलेगा, कमल खिलेगा"
A B.A.(English, Hindi & Sanskrit) & M.A.(Political Science) , he was the first head of government from outside the Congress party to serve a full five-year term, as a leader of the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP). A Padma Vibhushan awardee, he is also a recipient of India's highest civilian honour, the Bharat Ratna. Perceived to be an astute statesman and political leader, his birthday was declared 'Good Governance Day'. Vajpayee is also admired for his poetry.

  • Victory and defeat are a part of life,which are to be viewd with eqanimity.

   Here's a time line of Mr. Vajpayee's life :

  • 1924 - 25th December: Born in Gwalior, Madhya Pradesh, India
  • 1939 - Joined Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh(RSS)
  • 1942 - Participated in Quit India Movement
  • 1947 - Sangh Pracharak
  • 1951 - Associated with Bhartiya Jan Sangh
  • 1957 - Elected to Lok Sabha for first time from Balrampur, U.P
  • 1962 - Member Of Rajya Sabha
  • 1975 - Imprisoned in Bengaluru Jail during General Emergency
  • 1977 - 4th October: Delivered speech in Hindi in United Nations
  • 1980 - Founder Member of Bhartiya Janta Party
  • 1980 - Became First President of BJP
  • 1992 - Awarded Padma-Vibhushan
  • 1996 - 16th May: Elected PM for first time
  • 1998 - Pokhran Atomic Test done successfully in May
  • 1999 - Won the Kargil War
  • 1999 - 13th October: Elected PM for third time
  • 2001 - 14th July: Indo-Pak Summit, Agra
  • 2005 - Retired from Active Politics
  • 2015 - Awarded Bharat Ratna
  • 2018 - 16th August: Passed away in New Delhi at the age of 93
